Note
IMPORTANT: If sections are ever actually needed, the offset cannot actually be a byte array, but instead needs to be a union of the different types that the section might actually be. This is AUTOSAR compliant because the type field is the "tag", making the union a tagged union which is allowed. This will ensure that you can parse the different sections without having to do casts.
